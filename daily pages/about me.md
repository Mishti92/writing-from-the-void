i am none of the things I was until last year, or maybe i am more of it.

all i know is that i have a story to tell. movement has been my expression for as long as i can remember but lately, words are pouring out of me. i am not a writer but do I need a label to write. just one word after the other, it becomes a prose, a poem or an essay.

i am learning to be a human and so, you can expect me to write about the messiness and joy of being human.


I write mostly stream of conscious prose exploring the messiness and joy of being human. My work is rooted in anticapitalist sentiments and a great desire to unlearn the societal messaging that makes us believe we never HAVE enough and never ARE enough.
