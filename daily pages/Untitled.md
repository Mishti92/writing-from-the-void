**"Mental squeeze point"**

isn’t a widely recognized term, but it could describe a moment of **intense cognitive pressure**—when your brain feels overwhelmed, stuck, or constrained by competing thoughts, decisions, or mental fatigue.

It could show up as:

- The moment before a breakthrough when your mind is struggling to connect ideas.
- Creative block due to too much input or overthinking.
- Decision paralysis when multiple options feel equally compelling.
- The mental discomfort of pushing past old patterns or limits.

If you’re experiencing a mental squeeze point, movement (like a freestyle session), changing environments, or even just deep breathing can help release the tension and allow ideas to flow again. Do you feel this often in your creative process?
