##### **anatomy of a week:**

spend most days at home, minimal external input 

once or twice a week: 
- dress cute and head to a cafe for some writing/reading while enjoying a cup of chai ( *or as they call it chai latte* ). later walk to the beach and listen to the sound of the waves
- chai dates with a new or old friend

go out dancing once in 2/3 weeks 

##### **anatomy of a day:**

for an hour or two:
- chai and morning/evening pages 
- talk freely to camera for an hour, edit and upload right away. 
- unstructured learning, curiosity driven exploration
- organizing, sorting and developing the ideas and notes on the daily pages when you are in work mode, but not fully feeling it
- do nothing and get bored more often
- follow the voice to say yes, follow the voice to say no
- [midnight cravings](https://www.youtube.com/@ps_yourchica); record and share on youtube on the days you feel like. don't record or share on the days you don't feel like.
- slowly build a practice to become stronger in your body. start showing up for the health of your future self.
