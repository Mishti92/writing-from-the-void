I want you to know that i am happy we crossed paths  
that even if our time together were just those moments, your presence in my life meant something.

i will forever be grateful that i met you and was able to experience those beautiful memories with you.

I have started writing because you believed in me. and gave me the courage to believe in myself. I needed to hear it from someone else before i could tell it to myself. I wanted you to know that.

you helped me heal my heart in ways I wouldn’t even know about. I don’t know why I didn’t tell you that before.

you taught me what it meant to rest and allow myself to relax, feeling so safe and seen that I could let my guard down.

you saw something in me that I didn’t see in myself. but then somehow it became a part of me. and slowly I started seeing myself more clearly and deeply than ever before.

you made me want to face my fears and jump in the water. not directly, but in being with you, admiring the qualities I saw in you, I realized I was seeking that in myself.

you showed me a mirror to the person I was hoping to be but couldn’t see the face clearly of.

I don’t know what love means anymore but with what little I have come to understand, I think love might be these small moments that linger and become a part of you.

and by that logic, yes – I did love you, in its own special way.

and by loving you, I found a way to fall in love with myself.
