every time I find myself in a place of uncertainty, doubt, judgement or shame,  
I make a conscious decision to choose love over fear.  

to believe in love, no matter what.  
to believe in the magic of love, no matter what.  

no matter how any times the world makes me want to put up the walls.  
no matter how many times I almost lose hope and give into fear.  
no matter how many times my surrender is met with doubt and uncertainty on the other end.  
no matter how many times my love is met with fear.  
again and again, I will choose love over fear.
