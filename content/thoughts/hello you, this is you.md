hello you,

this is *you*

you are the current me. sometimes you may also be the past me. you can't tell it fully yet. you often holds some beliefs and patterns that are blocking the view of the path. until you clear those patterns and understand where those beliefs are coming, you can't see the path clearly. Because those beliefs. those patters ( give it a name ), they are masking the real *you*. would you be brave enough to remove these blockers, cross the river, face the fears, face the discomfort. face the pain. face the grief. face the rage. face being misunderstood. face being wrong. face being right. to reveal the real *you*. 

*you* is the future me, that holds the answers, and sends clues to you in the form of nudges, as the voice of the universe, as the sudden sparks or curiosities.

listen to your body closely and you may be able to identify it more often. the calmness of breath or the shaking of legs. the rising of heart beat or the feeling of being light. the way the eyes lit up or go dim. the way the body moves and the mind follows. 

the way the world just stops when you see glimpses of *you*.

your body is always sending you the signals. if you are too disconnected from your body, if you don't tend or care for it, if you don't come back to your body, your *home* everyone once in a while, you are already too far away from the path. and the signal gets broken. and the mind moves, the body follows.

so come back to your body. listen to it. listen to the voice. the voice of *you*, enlightening the path for you. 

will you be brave enough to listen to *you*.

write for 30 days. move for 30 days. make art for 30 days. work on your idea for 30 days. believe in your curiosity for 30 days. 

post on substack for 30 days. post on instagram for 30 days. for on medium for 30 days. show up for 30 days.

try something for 30 days, learn something for 30 days, fail at something for 30 days, allow yourself to be wrong for 30 days, allow yourself to do everything wrong for 30 days.

you hear me? you are not allowed to give up. you didn't come all this way to pack up your curiosities and stop believing in your creativity, in your dreams, in your *mansha*, in your calling.

you are doing it for the first time and no matter what the rule book says, or what society blueprint says, or what has worked or not worked for someone else.

you have to eventually figure it out for yourself, do the work and one step at a time. you do that work now, or next year, or 5 years form now, or 10 years from now, you choose. 

but remember, 
[[o amanhã não está garantido]]

in all the you's, you are the only *you*. 

everything that has happened to you, for you, has made you the person you are today. your childhood, your interests, your curiosities, your weird kinks, your jokes, your talent, your skills, that one thing that makes you *you*, the way you love, the way you care, the way you hold space, the way you make chai, the way you enjoy making pasta, the way you smile. all of that makes you *you*.

without a mask. without a filter. without doubting yourself. without playing small. without the gates of shame. without fear holding you back. without the boundaries of guilt. without the chains of judgement. 

the *you* already knows who it wants to *be*. not the superficial be, but the *be* at the core, deep deep within. 

you just have to listen to its voice and follow the path that leads to *you*.

be aware of the nudges you get at times, making you feel that something is not quite right. something needs to be changed. maybe something more. maybe something less. but something. you don't know what that something is yet. 

or maybe there is a curiosity to explore the unknown. you must receive and thank the voice. handle the curiosity with care. give it your utmost attention and clear the space for it to enter and make itself known. 

you must acknowledge the curiosity. you must acknowledge that the desire. the desire for change. for something more. or maybe something less. the desire is coming from within you. 

from *you*. 

and you must believe that this change is possible. that it can happen for you too. you must believe that you have everything you need to make that change happen. you have to be brave. you have to be brave enough to say yes without any reassurance of the outcome. you don't know the outcome. you can't control the outcome. you can only control what you do and your actions.

you must believe in your ability to do the right thing. to choose the right path. you must place trust in your intuition. trust in your story to reveal what is meant for you. trust in the timing of the universe.  

but dear you, you must be brave enough to say yes. give yourself 30 days to follow your intuition, your desire, your curiosity.

for 30 days. 

and let it reveal itself to you. don't try to control the outcome. trust in the magic of universe, in the magic of life, in the magic of existence, that you don't know everything yet. and you will never know everything ever. you are always learning. always evolving. always exploring.

your experiences shape how you interact with the world. one day at a time. your past dictates a story that may either hold you back or empower you to step into your own power.

what if your curiosity is the key that reveals the bridge between the two, the path that leads you to *you*.
