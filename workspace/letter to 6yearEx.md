*written on my journal, not sent*

28 September, 2023
2 o'clock 

Hi it's been exactly seven days since we last texted

I don't want to assume. How have you been managing but since you haven't made any effort to send a text or reconcile, I will respect your peace and give you time and space to move on.

I wish I was as strong and as emotionally as detached as you are. I haven't been doing OK. I cry almost every day. I try to show to mom and dad that I am OK and be busy in my work and hide my emotions, but often I go to the bathroom and cry in silence. then I wipe my tears and I come back to work.

You have made me feel that I was right to be anxious in this relationship. that I was never enough for you. never good looking enough. never successful enough or whatever your criteria is of a good partner. I understand you have a commitment and communication issues, but it sucks to be on the other side of this.

I feel betrayed that someone took away six years of my life and with it a big part of me, maybe I'm still mad at you for everything, but I also know nothing can be fixed by going in the past.

We both made our choices. I want to text you so badly and not let you go, but this relationship has been one sided forever. 

Since the first day I kissed you and every day since then I pushed myself in your life and you never valued my presence. Maybe I am exaggerating these stories in my head, but this is how it feels.

I am mad at you. Why did you not end it? Why did you give me false hope. now I have last seven years of memories to heal and fix all the broken pieces. 

I'm used to it though I was stupid to believe this would be any better. I've learned to love myself and not be dependent on anyone and I don't know if I will ever love like this again.

I say that, but then I'm also the first one to love and give so much. Maybe that's the problem. I shouldn't love this hard.

I hope I get the strength to not text you as you said 
*"let it just be over for once and all, and no going back"*.

I will tend to my broken heart in the meantime and prioritize my happiness. You never deserved my love. I should have realized that sooner, but I was stupid. I loved you so much. I thought by giving more and more love would make you love me too. And I am done crying today. 

I'll go back to work and push those feelings even further till next time. I break down. 

I still love you.
