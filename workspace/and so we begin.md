the time leading up to the breakup and the 6 months that follow are not pretty. I was in a frozen state the entire time. 

now as I am reading those texts and journal entries for the first time, I want to do nothing but run to old me and hug her. she was going through an unbearable pain, something she couldn’t even articulate to herself. as I open this story for others to witness, my hope is to raise awareness around these unspoken feelings and the deep emptiness that I would feel within me forever. 

this is, but, one part of the story and more so, it’s my side of the story. I do not claim to say any of this is right or wrong, just what this chica could do at that time, or wanted to do, what her reflections were, what her learning were and how she is learning along the way. i don't want to go through this alone anymore. and I dont want anyone else to ever go through this on their own. 

The screenshots I shared last night happened around sept 20, 2023. what you are seeing are the exchanges, that were fuelled by anger and fear from deep deep within me.  more was happening behind the scenes, somethings that I shared in my journal entries but somethings that I felt ashamed of and didn't even acknowledge to myself. As I am reflecting back on that time, it is important that I remain honest and true to myself, and then I can decide how much of that truth to filter out to share with the world outside of me. 

Sept 25, I received my new journal that I had ordered from amazon. Sept 26, I wrote my first journal entry and the second entry would be the letter to 6yearEx ( fourth post on this account ). 

This letter was not shared with him. I ask myself why?
